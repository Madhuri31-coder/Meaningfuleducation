# Business Scenario

## Objective
Implement centralized material governance using SAP MDG and distribute approved records to connected systems through IDOCs.

## Process Flow
1. User creates material in MDG
2. Workflow triggers approval
3. Validation checks execute
4. Material activates after approval
5. IDOC generated automatically
6. Target systems receive data

## Benefits
- Improved data quality
- Centralized governance
- Reduced manual maintenance
- Faster replication
- Better compliance
